About

Dr. Kunal Manna is an EPSRC Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Warwick Manufacturing Group, University of Warwick. He specializes in polymer nanocomposites, EMI shielding materials, and sustainable polymer recycling. He holds a PhD and M.Sc. in Chemistry from IIT Kharagpur and has extensive teaching and research experience.

  • Construct of Carbonaceous nanostructures over IPN for EMI Shielding
