About

Dr. Kunal Manna is an EPSRC Postdoctoral Fellow at the Warwick Manufacturing Group (WMG), University of Warwick, UK. He completed his PhD from IIT Kharagpur and works on polymer nanocomposites, vitrimer chemistry and EMI shielding.

Research Interests

  • Data-driven plastic recycling
  • Sustainable recycling & upcycling using vitrimer chemistry
  • Conducting polymer nanocomposites for EMI shielding
  • IPN membranes for water purification
